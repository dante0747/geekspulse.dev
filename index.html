Why GeeksPulse?

32 Curated Feeds
Hand-picked from trusted engineering blogs, security advisories, open-source communities, and technical news sources. No clickbait, no hot takes.
No Ad Trackers
No ad tracking pixels. No ads. No VC money. Your reading habits stay yours. GeeksPulse is funded by coffee and optional PayPal support.
Refreshed Regularly
Article data is rebuilt from live RSS feeds via a scheduled GitHub Actions pipeline. The feed health panel shows exactly when it last ran and which feeds succeeded.
Open Source
Every line of GeeksPulse is public on GitHub. See the feed list, audit the build script, or open a PR to add your favourite RSS source.
